Welding Experts

As TIG welding specialists, we pride ourselves on delivering precise and high-quality welds for a variety of applications. Our expertise doesn't stop there; we also excel in MIG welding, known for its versatility and speed, and dual shield welding, which combines the best of both flux-cored and gas-shielded processes to handle even the most challenging projects. Our team of experienced professionals is well-versed in all welding techniques, ensuring that we can meet any welding need with the highest level of skill and craftsmanship. Whether it's TIG, MIG, or dual shield, we are committed to providing exceptional welding services that meet the diverse requirements of our clients.

Pharmaceutical

In the pharmaceutical industry, precision and reliability are paramount, and our welding services are tailored to meet these exacting standards. We specialize in Hastelloy tank welding, providing corrosion-resistant solutions essential for pharmaceutical applications. Hastelloy, known for its excellent chemical resistance and durability, is ideal for welding tanks and equipment used in the pharmaceutical manufacturing process. Our skilled welders ensure that every weld is meticulously executed, maintaining the integrity and cleanliness required in pharmaceutical environments. Whether it's constructing new Hastelloy tanks or repairing existing equipment, our expertise guarantees high-quality, reliable results that comply with stringent industry regulations. Trust us to deliver exceptional welding services that support the critical needs of the pharmaceutical sector.

Custom Vehicle and Offroad

Our services include designing and fabricating robust suspension systems, roll cages, and bed cages that enhance the performance and safety of your vehicle. Whether you require custom-built off-road components or modifications to existing vehicles, our skilled team uses the latest techniques and high-quality materials to ensure durability and reliability under the toughest conditions. From concept to completion, we work closely with you to understand your specific requirements and deliver solutions that exceed expectations. Our expertise extends to a wide range of off-road racing vehicles, providing tailored modifications and enhancements that give you the competitive edge. Trust us to transform your vehicle into a high-performance machine capable of conquering the most challenging terrains.
